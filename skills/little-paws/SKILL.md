# Little Paws - Updated with Close-Up Shots

## MANDATORY: Use Close-Up/Medium-Close Shots

Based on user's reference image, ALWAYS use:
- **CLOSE-UP composition** - heads prominent, head-to-midbody framing
- **Characters LARGE in frame** - heads take up significant space
- **Minimal background** - white space with simple horizontal floor line only
- Two-shot with both heads near top third

## Character Details (from reference)

### Male (bigger)
- Broad face, bulkier body
- Dark brown/black eyes with ONE white highlight
- Thick collar with LARGE ROUND PENDANT (~25% head width, blue/teal)
- Sometimes half-lidded/mischievous eyes
- Short fur tufts, small speckle marks

### Female (smaller)
- Rounder, softer features
- Large emerald-green eyes with ONE white highlight
- Necklace of SMALL BEADS (~10-12% head width each)
- More open, wide-eyed expressions
- Stronger cheek blush

## Art Style
- Thick clean black outlines
- Flat colors, minimal shading
- Small heart icons for affection
- Simple motion lines
- Three whiskers per side, pink nose/inner ears

## Text Display
- Show narrator text as caption ABOVE panel or speech bubble BELOW
- Keep text small and unobtrusive

## Workflow
1. Use generate_story.py to create prompts
2. Generate 4 scenes with close-up framing
3. Compose 2x2 grid
4. Create video (9:16, 2.5s/frame)
5. Share via bore
