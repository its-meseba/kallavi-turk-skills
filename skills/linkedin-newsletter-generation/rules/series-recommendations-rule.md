# Series Post Recommendations Rule

**For series posts only.**

---

## Rule

Whenever creating content for a series, always create/update a `recommendations.md` file in the series folder.

## Purpose

Recommendations are for exploration purposes only. They do not affect the current post or series structure. They are exploratory notes for future posts.

## Format

```markdown
# Series Recommendations

## Exploratory Recommendations

### Category 1: [Topic Area]
- **Recommendation**: [Brief description]
- **Rationale**: Why this could be valuable

### Category 2: [Topic Area]
- **Recommendation**: [Brief description]
- **Rationale**: Why this could be valuable
```

## Guidelines

1. **Ultrathink**: Deeply consider the series topic and potential directions
2. **Categorical organization**: Group recommendations by topic area
3. **Brief rationale**: Explain why each recommendation could be valuable
4. **Exploratory only**: These are seeds for future exploration, not commitments
5. **Append**: Add to existing recommendations.md rather than replacing

## Output Location

`series/[series-slug]/recommendations.md`
